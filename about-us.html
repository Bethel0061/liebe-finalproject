Liebeshände is dedicaed to improving the lives of children from Myanmar who have been displayed by conflict. Our mission is to provide essential education, healthcare and jouful experiences to nuture their growth and well-being.

Founded in 2024, Liebeshände was born out of a desire to make a difference in the lives of children affected by the ongoing crisis in Myanmar. Our team of passionate volunteers works tirelessly to bring hope and support to these vulnerable communities.

Meet the dedicated individuals behind Liebeshände each bringing their unique skills and compassion to our mission.
